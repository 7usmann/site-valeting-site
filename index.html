About

A small, friendly team — not a franchise

SITE Valeting is a small mobile valeting team covering Stevenage — reviewers consistently mention "the lads" or "the team" turning up, rather than a single one-man operation. That means jobs get done properly and quickly, with more than one pair of hands on the car.

Being mobile is the whole point — we come to your home or workplace rather than you having to drive anywhere and wait around. Customers consistently mention the same things: punctuality, attention to detail, clear pricing with no surprises, and cars coming out looking showroom-new. Several reviewers have already come back for repeat bookings.
